Audience Insights · Interactive

Are the Eras Real? Mapping Taylor Swift's Sound

Everyone talks about Taylor Swift's "eras" like they're different artists, so I tested it: 582 songs mapped by sound alone with PCA and clustering. Explore the interactive map, click any song to find its cross-era "sonic twins," and watch her whole catalog pivot in 2020. The clusters double as a practical tool for playlist curation, catalog marketing and audience targeting.

Steinway & Fender: Heritage Brands in a Digital Age

How do century-old brands stay iconic when everything around them goes digital? An independent study of two legendary instrument makers, one uncompromisingly analog and one going direct-to-consumer, and what each says about heritage, positioning and changing consumers.

Sonic Branding · Independent Paper

Kingdom Hearts: One Melody, Twenty Years

Nobody can summarize the plot of Kingdom Hearts, yet everyone can tell you exactly how it feels. This paper argues the franchise's coherence is musical: one piano theme, rearranged across twenty years of games, doing the brand-identity work the storytelling cannot.

About

I trained as a classical pianist at the Boyer College of Music, then carried the same discipline (pattern, structure, endless practice) into business school. At INSEAD I studied what makes brands beloved, what makes audiences care, and how good ideas actually get executed.

I'm happiest turning research into clear, visual stories that teams can act on, whether that's a brand-strategy study, an audience analysis, or short-form content I create myself on TikTok.

I'm also thoroughly global: I've lived, worked and studied across the US, France, Singapore, Japan and Korea, and I'm at my best on international teams and projects. Currently looking for roles in comms, marketing and strategy across industries, though music will always be my soft spot.
